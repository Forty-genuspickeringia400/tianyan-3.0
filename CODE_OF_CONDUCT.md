# Code of Conduct

## Our standard
We want Tianyan 3.0 to be a constructive, technically serious, and respectful open-source space.

## Expected behavior
- be respectful and specific
- criticize ideas, not people
- keep discussions grounded in evidence
- avoid exaggerating unverified claims
- help improve docs, tests, and reproducibility

## Unacceptable behavior
- harassment or personal attacks
- bad-faith misrepresentation
- posting secrets or private credentials
- abusive or discriminatory language

## Enforcement
Maintainers may moderate discussions, close threads, or reject contributions that violate these principles.
